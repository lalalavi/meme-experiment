In this study, you will be interacting with a platform that is meant to resemble aspects of social media (for example, Instagram).


You can either post “Memes” (humorous pictures) or see and rate Memes posted by other Users in the Feed. In an earlier part of this study, these memes were rated with a like or dislike by 40 independent participants. Thus, the feedback you will receive for your posts is real. This feedback will be displayed to you in the form of likes or dislikes, after you select to post. You rate the Memes posted by other Users by giving them a like or a dislike.
