Please read carefully the information below. At the end of this instructions, you will be asked some comprehension questions to make sure they were understood properly.


In this study, you will be interacting within a platform that is meant to resemble aspects of social media (for example, Instagram). You can either post “Memes” (humorous pictures) or see and rate Memes in the Feed.

This is a preview of how the platform will look like:

You can post Memes whenever you want by pressing the "POST" button. Each time you decide to post a Meme, you select among a set of six potentially funny, interesting or relatable Meme pictures. Please select the Meme you like the best and that you think the others will like as well. You select a meme by PRESSING A NUMBER IN YOUR KEYBOARD.

In an earlier part of this study, these memes were rated by 40 independent participants. Thus, the feedback you will receive after you post is real. The maximum number of Likes that a post received during the ratings was 34, and the minimum was 13.
