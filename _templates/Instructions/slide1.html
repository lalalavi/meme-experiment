Each time you decide to post, you select among a set of 6 potentially funny, interesting or relatable Memes. Please select the Meme you like the best and that you think the others will like as well.

You select a meme by pressing the button below the image.

In an earlier part of this study, these memes were rated by 40 independent participants. Thus, the feedback you will receive after you post is real.

The maximum number of Likes that a post received during the ratings was 34, and the minimum was 6.
