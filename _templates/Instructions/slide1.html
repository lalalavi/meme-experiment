In this experiment you will:

  • Be able to post or see as many Memes you want for 10 minutes. The figure to the right shows a preview of this platform. The left part displays the Feed.
  • Each time you decide to post a Meme, you select among a set of six potentially funny, interesting or relatable Meme pictures. Your last post will be displayed in the right part of the screen.
  • In an earlier part of this study, these memes were rated by 40 independent participants. Thus, the feedback you will receive after you post is real.
  • The maximum number of Likes that a post received during the ratings was 34, and the minimum was 13.
