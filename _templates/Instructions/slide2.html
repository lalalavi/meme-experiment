In an earlier part of this study, these memes were rated by 40 independent participants. Thus, the feedback you will receive after you post is real.

The maximum number of Likes that a post received during the ratings was 34, and the minimum was 6.

After you have selected a Meme, you are asked to add three informative "tags" (short keywords) related to this Meme. These tags might be used for the data analysis of the study. It is therefore important that your tags are as clear and descriptive as possible.

However, this step not required to post an image. You are free to do this step as you would be in real social media.
