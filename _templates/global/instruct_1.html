In the study, you can post “Memes” (humorous pictures) and rate Memes posted by the other Users. You rate the Memes posted by other Users by giving a “Like” if you enjoy someone’s post. If you do not enjoy it, you can just wait for another User's post. In turn, other Users can Like your posts.


You can post Memes whenever you want. Each time you decide to post a Meme, you select among a set of potentially funny, interesting or relatable Meme pictures. Please select the Meme you like the best and that you think the others will like as well.

When you're ready to continue, press Next.
