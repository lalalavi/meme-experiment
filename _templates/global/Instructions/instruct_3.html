After you have selected the tags, your post is ready and will be shown to the other Users. Now each of the other Users have the opportunity to Like your post if they want to.


When all other Users have decided whether or not they Like your post, you will see the number of Likes your post received. The maximum number of Likes a post can receive is 19 (that is, 1 Like per User).



When you're ready to continue, press Next.
